The NAACL Student Research Workshop (SRW) 2025 will be held in conjunction with NAACL 2025 in Albuquerque, New Mexico, from April 30 – May 1.

The SRW gives student researchers in Computational Linguistics and NLP the opportunity to present their work and receive constructive feedback and mentorship by experienced members of the ACL community.

My thesis proposal was accepted to this workshop and honored as the Best Thesis Proposal. I presented my work at the conference.
